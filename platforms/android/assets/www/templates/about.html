Vaanoli is simple, super fast collection of tamil internet radios. Listen to your favorite radios from anywhere and anytime!

Vaanoli Supports:

  • Fast buffering
  • Stopping when you receive incoming calls
  • Reducing volume when you receive any notifications
  • Playing nice with other applications
  • Off timer
  • Favorite radios
  • Background playing
  • Stopping when you unplug head phone
  • And many of your favorite radios

DISCLAIMER: Vaanoli does not host any radios, it simply provides links to the radios hosted in the internet. If you are owner of the radio and you don't want Vaanoli to provide link to your radio then please send an email to the address below.
